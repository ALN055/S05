Superstring Dimension

Superstring Dimension is an Abyss-variant gamemode that was added in Version 4.2. It is currently accessible only by players with Lv. 81 or above.

Superstring Dimension's cycle has 4 stages: Fringe (left), Intensive (top), Perilous (right) and Singular (bottom). Players will start challenging Fringe in order to get access to Intensive and Singular. Successfully completing the former will unlock Perilous, which is basically a boss stage.

Fringe, Intensive and Perilous may have same weather, while Singular has different one from the rest. All four may also have different effects to each other.

Completing Fringe, Intensive and Singular will rewards 6000 points each, and Perilous will give 3000 + remaining points, which will be based on how long they take to succeed.

- Ranks

Unlike other Abyss-variants, Superstring Dimension introduces Trophies mechanic which, depends on player's place during cycle's finalization, will be either increased or decreased. The change of remaining trophies will promote them to higher rank if they have high enough trophies, or vice versa. In other words, players will not be promoted or relegated directly.

Once entering the mode for the first time, player will also start with 180 Virtual Trophies (which can be increased by 100 if promoted to Red Lotus once), which will be reduced instead of the actual trophies in case of their rank is low during finalization.

- Weathers

  • Physical: Enemies take 50% bonus Physical DMG and 50% less Lightning DMG.
  • Lightning: Enemies take 50% bonus Lightning DMG and 50% less Physical DMG.
  • Fire: Enemies take 50% bonus Fire DMG and 50% less Ice DMG.
  • Frost: Enemies take 50% more Ice DMG and 50% less Fire DMG.
  • Ranged: Enemies take 50% bonus ranged DMG and 50% less melee DMG.
  • Scrappy: Enemies take 50% bonus melee DMG and 50% less ranged DMG.
  • Counter: Type countering effects are 20% stronger.
  • Quantum: QUA Valkyries deal 20% bonus DMG.
  • IMG Environment: IMG Valkyries deal 20% bonus DMG.
  • Electrified: Enemies take 40% bonus damage when paralyzed and within 4s after the Paralyze ends.
  • Freezing Environment: Enemies take 40% bonus damage when Frozen and within 4s after the Frozen duration ends.
  • Ignited: Enemies take 40% bonus damage when Ignited and Ignite damage increases by 45%.
  • Blood Thorn Patch: Enemies take 40% bonus damage when Bleeding and Bleed damage increases by 45%.
  • Bloodthirsty: Enemy kills boost Total DMG by 20% for 15s. 8 stacks max. Every stack has an independent timer.
  • Fortified: Enemies are shielded but they take 40% bonus DMG when their shields are broken.




Memorial Arena

Memorial Arena, as its name implies, is a game mode where Captains can face off against challenging bosses from previous encounters. The virtual battlefield uses data to recreate enemies and have them battle with Valkyries with the aim of increasing Valkyries' battle prowess. Memorial Arena runs from Tuesday, 04:00 to Monday, 02:00 every week. Between Monday 02:00 and Tuesday 04:00, results are calculated and rewards are distributed.

- Battling Enemies

Enemies in the Memorial Arena have their memory upgraded with ranks "C, B, A, S, SS" with each letter corresponding to a different difficulty level. Once a Captain has defeated an enemy at a certain difficulty level, they will automatically move up to the next difficulty level. After certain difficulty levels, enemies in the arena will begin to use different attacks and sometimes even special ultimate skills when damaged enough.

The Exalted bracket also features 2 SSS rank Bosses (as of Patch v6.1, including 20% UP one, and one at the middle) which the captain can challenge to get bonus score-based rewards. In addition, there's also Buffed Mode that can be enabled before the encounter. That will overall benefit their team during the battle, in exchange for the score loss at the end of the fight.

- Locked Gear & Valkyrie

Once a valkyrie or a gear is used for a Memorial Arena boss, the valkyrie or gear is locked to that boss. In addition, unawaken and awaken forms of the same valkyrie cannot be used for the same boss -- even if they are used in different attempts. The lock is finalized when an attempt is registered -- ie. by either defeating a boss or being defeated without exiting early.

- Brackets

Captains are placed into 4 different brackets based on their captain level.
  • Basic (Lv.38-55): 2 bosses; 4 attempts per day.
  • Elites (Lv.56~69): 2 bosses; 4 attempts per day.
  • Masters (Lv.70-80): 3 bosses; 5 attempts per day.
  • Exalted (Lv.81-88): 3 bosses; 6 attempts per day.
If a Captain levels up during a rotation, they will not move up in bracket until the next rotation. The difficulty of the bosses increase significantly as a captain moves up in brackets. For example, a Rank S boss may pose a significant challenge for a level 70 captain in Masters even though the captain can achieve good scores with same boss at Rank SS previously in Elites. The additional 3rd boss may be out of reach due the sudden requirement for a 3rd team of valkyries along with their gears. Memorial Arena is thus one of the reasons that it is advisable to slow down leveling above level 50 or level 60 -- depending on active events of the time.

- Scoring

The maximum score for each boss goes up as a captain beats a higher rank of that boss. The actual score is reduced by the amount of in-game arena time the captain spends beating the boss of that rank (Time spent on previous rank are not counted). At SS rank, every in-game second spent equates to 53-point reduction, and each SS rank boss allows for a maximum score of 31946 which equivalent to defeating them with 0-second wasted. Captains can challenge the same boss again at SS or SSS (Exalted only) rank. Only the highest score achieved will be counted.

Each rotation there is also one boss that gives a bonus 20% to the score achieved against it.

During a boss encounter, if a captain is defeated either by losing all valkyries or by timer running out, the captain will lose a challenge attempt. A score based on the remaining health of the boss and its rank will be awarded -- although much less than the score from defeating the boss. If the captain abandons during an encounter, no score is awarded but no challenge attempt is consumed either.

Elysian Realm

Elysian Realm is similar to Threshold Breach, you solo the entire stage with one combat Valkyrie and two support Valkyries that have special skills to aid you in battle. The only difference is that you use your own Valkyrie, and not a trial Valkyrie.

- Gameplay

There are 4 difficulties: Safe, Normal, Dangerous, and Real. Safe has 8 floors, Normal has 12 floors, Dangerous has 16 floors, and Real also has 16 floors. Clearing Safe, Normal, or Dangerous with each combat Valkyrie for the first time gives 100 crystals. For example, clearing Safe for the first time with HoR and clearing Safe for the first time with SN both give separate first time clearing rewards, so you would get 200 crystals in this example. To unlock a difficulty you need to complete all floors of the one before it. For example, to unlock Normal, you need to clear all of Sequence: Safe.

You can adjust the difficulty level when you play Real by selecting different handicaps. The higher the difficulty the more rewards. The starting difficulty is 30. You can unlock more handicaps after clearing Real at higher difficulties.

- Signets/Buffs

After clearing a floor, you will get a Signet. Each member of Fire Moth has their own Signets that gives unique buffs. Fire Moth members are also referred to as Flame-Chaser. The **t floor always gives Elysia's Signet and after that you choose from 2 or 3 portals at the end of the stage for which buffs you want. Each portal has a symbol which indicates which Signets you can obtain from there.

- List of Signets

  • Elysia: Signets of Ego
    • Elysia gives buffs that are unique for your chosen combat Valkyrie.

  • Hua: Signets of Vicissitude
    • Hua gives buffs that start stack up over time and for not getting hit. The amount of stacks you have are shown above your HP bar.

  • Kevin: Signets of Deliverance
    • Kevin gives buffs for using your Ultimate.

  • Kalpas: Signets of Decimation
    • Kalpas gives buffs related to HP and HP loss.

  • Sakura: Signets of Setsuna
    • Sakura gives buffs for evasion.

  • Eden: Signets of Gold
    • Eden gives buffs related to SP.

  • Su: Signets of Bodhi
    • Su gives buffs related to Combo Hits.

  • Mobius: Signets of Infinity
    • Mobius gives buffs for summoned entities, support Valkyries, and ELFs.

  • Aponia: Signets of Discipline
    • Aponia gives buffs related to conditionals.

  • Kosma: Signets of Daybreak
    • Kosma gives buffs on basic atks, evasions,ultimate which deals DoT.

  • Vill-V: Signets of Helix
    • Vill-V gives buffs on weapon active uses.

  • Griseo: Signets of Stars
    • Griseo gives buffs related to using ultimates,evasion, and weapon active.

  • Pardofelis: Signets of Reverie
    • Pardofelis gives buffs based on currency gained.
